TEAM 01 · SPORTSBOOK
Sportsbook Core
PM: Marcos  ·  EM: Mauri  ·  Lu
Sportsbook operates as one mission with two execution lanes.
Problem
  • Experienced bettors need more flexibility and reliability: market depth, combinations, cashout, live quality, and provider resilience.
  • Brazil needs the shared sportsbook spine to fit local behavior: first-bet speed, market confidence, sports hierarchy, and product trust.
Objective
  • OSB Checkout owns the layer underneath every bet placed in Draftea. Stability first: platform migrations and Custom Bet hardening protect the surfaces that drive most GMV and unblock Early Cashout and new offer expansion downstream.
  • On top of that floor: Personalization puts the right bet in front of each user at each moment. Search covers what the Home can't predict. The strategic bet is MTS: combine all our offer, anytime, with adequate pricing — without renting that capability from BetRadar.
How we win
  • Platform stability and Custom Bet hardening — no GMV-driving surface breaks.
  • Early Cashout: available when the user needs it, not when the provider allows it.
  • MTS: own our pricing and correlation capability — end BetRadar dependency.
  • Personalization: right bet, right user, right moment.
  • Search and discovery that covers what the Home can't predict.
TEAM 02 · SPORTSBOOK
Sportsbook Experience
PM: André  ·  EM: Furlan
Sportsbook operates as one mission with two execution lanes.
Problem
  • Users still complain that Draftea doesn't have the offer they find at other bookmakers — they don't concentrate their share of wallet with us. We're leaving money on the table.

  • Increase GMV through more offer, a best-in-class live experience, and features that incentivize betting — such as ghost legs and other engagement mechanics.

How we win
  • Expand offer breadth: close the gaps users flag vs competitor bookmakers.
  • Best-in-class live experience: real-time, fluid, always-on — more bets per session.

  • Offer quality and coverage that makes Draftea the primary book, not a secondary one.
