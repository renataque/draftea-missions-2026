TEAM 01 · SPORTSBOOK
Sportsbook
PM: Joao  ·  Marcos  ·  André   |   EM: Lu  ·  Mauri  ·  Furlan
Problem
  • To become the best sportsbook in LATAM we face two barriers: market parity — best offer quality, odds, and flexibility to build tickets at any moment — and differentiation as the most premium OSB in MX and BR: simple, innovative, best-in-class.
  • Market/bet option gaps are the #2 churn driver (7.2% → 9.0% Apr). Crear Apuesta limits causing active churn for 2+ months. Low spenders at 18.3% D7 retention.
  • To win, we must invest heavily in our own probability, correlation, cashout, and risk management — reducing hard provider dependencies that keep us as a second-tier product.
Objective
  • Deliver the best sports betting experience in LATAM, consistently across both countries.
  • Become #1 in Mexico and establish the foundation to be the most recognized OSB brand in Brazil — achieving $33M USD NGR in H2.
How we win
  • Best multiprovider platform: smart routing, auto-failover and catalog consistency.
  • Bet anything, combine anything, feel nothing loading — seamless ticket creation with no limits. Behind the scenes, risk is always under control.
  • Best cashout in the market: availability >90% of tickets and >90% of match time.
  • Replicate MX transparency and product taste into BR — close the post-bet experience gap.
  • Hit $33M USD NGR in H2 — MX $21M, BR $12M.
